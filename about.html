Meet Joy

Joy Alexandria Miller, LPC-S

Licensed Professional Counselor-Supervisor
PhD Candidate in Clinical Psychology
EMDR-Trained Trauma Therapist

Healing isn't one-size-fits-all — and neither am I.

Welcome! If you've landed here, you've already done something brave: you've decided your well-being is worth investing in. I don't take that lightly, and I'd be honored to walk the rest of the way with you.

I'm Joy — therapist, clinical supervisor, doctoral candidate, and firm believer that every person carries the capacity to heal, grow, and thrive, even when life has suggested otherwise.

Therapy With Me

I take a personalized, integrative approach, tailoring therapy to your unique needs and goals.

Drawing from Solution-Focused Therapy, CBT, DBT, Gestalt techniques, and EMDR trauma treatment, I help clients gain insight, build practical skills, and create meaningful change.

Areas of Focus

Trauma Recovery

Helping clients process the past so it no longer controls the present.

Substance Use & Recovery

Compassionate, judgment-free support for lasting change.

Couples & Families

Strengthening communication, connection, and trust.

LPC Supervision

Becoming a great therapist takes more than passing an exam—it takes mentorship, support, and real-world experience.

As a Board-Approved LPC Supervisor with experience in clinical leadership, I provide supervision that goes beyond collecting hours.

  • Clinical case consultation
  • Ethics and documentation guidance
  • Case conceptualization
  • Professional identity development
  • Individualized mentorship
