Counseling, Supervision & Training

Supportive, compassionate services designed to meet you where you are— whether you're seeking personal healing, stronger relationships, professional supervision, or continuing education.

Individual Counseling

Life gets heavy — you don’t have to carry it alone. Whether you’re working through trauma, anxiety, depression, substance use, or simply feeling stuck, individual counseling gives you a dedicated space to be fully heard and fully supported.

Together, we’ll uncover the root of what’s weighing on you and build practical strategies to help you move forward with clarity and confidence.

Book a Consultation

Couples Counseling

Every relationship hits rough patches — the difference is what you do next.

Couples counseling helps you and your partner break unhealthy cycles, communicate with honesty and compassion, and rebuild the connection that brought you together.

Whether you’re navigating conflict, rebuilding trust, or simply wanting to grow stronger together, this is a space where both voices matter.

Book a Consultation

Family Counseling

Families are complicated — and worth fighting for.

Family counseling brings everyone to the table to improve communication, heal old wounds, and strengthen the bonds that hold you together.

From parent-child conflict to life transitions and blended family dynamics, we’ll work toward a home where everyone feels seen, heard, and valued.

Book a Consultation

LPC-Associate Supervision

Your hours should build more than a license — they should build a career.

As a Board-Approved LPC Supervisor with clinical directorship experience, I offer supervision that goes beyond the checklist: sharpened case conceptualization, ethical decision-making, documentation skills, and the confidence to step into the field as the therapist you’re meant to be.

Individual and group supervision available.

Schedule Supervision Consultation

CEU Trainings

Continuing education shouldn’t put you to sleep.

My CEU trainings are engaging, practical, and designed for real-world application — covering topics like trauma-informed care, ethics, couples work, and clinical best practices.

Perfect for individual clinicians, group practices, and agencies looking to invest in their teams.
